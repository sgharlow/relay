# Relay — logo generation prompt (Gemini / Imagen)

Brand palette (matches the app UI + the intro/outro cards in this folder):
deep navy `#0B1020` · owner-blue `#5B9CFF` · warm amber `#F5B14C` · teal accent `#49E0A0`.

---

## Primary prompt (symbol mark, no text)

> Design a minimalist, modern flat-vector app logo for a product called "Relay" — a
> secure "living-continuity" vault that passes trusted access from one person to another
> in an emergency. Central symbol: two abstract rounded forms passing a glowing
> baton/spark between them, like a relay handoff, the negative space subtly forming the
> letter "R". Use a deep navy background (#0B1020) with a smooth gradient from owner-blue
> (#5B9CFF) on the left form to warm amber (#F5B14C) on the right form, plus a thin teal
> (#49E0A0) accent on the spark. Clean geometric line weights, generous negative space,
> centered composition, crisp edges, subtle inner glow, no gradient banding. Style:
> enterprise security/fintech, trustworthy and calm, not playful. Flat vector, app-icon
> ready, on a 1:1 square. No text, no drop shadows, no photorealism, no clutter.

---

## Tips

- **Generate the symbol first (no text), square 1:1.** It's your favicon / app icon and
  the hardest part. Image models misspell — set any wordmark in a real font afterward.
- **Wordmark version:** append — *"...include the lowercase wordmark 'relay' in a clean
  geometric sans-serif to the right of the mark, same height as the symbol."*
- **Cleaner icon:** add *"on a transparent background"* or *"flat color, no background
  gradient."*
- **Variation that scales better to 32px:** swap the two-figures motif for *"an
  interlocking pair of arcs forming a continuous loop, owner-blue to amber"* (reads as
  continuity / handoff).

---

## Alternate concepts to try

1. **State-machine motif** — *"a single rounded arrow that loops from a locked padlock to
   an open one and back, owner-blue to amber gradient, suggesting ARMED → RELEASED →
   ARMED; flat vector, navy ground."*
2. **Shielded handoff** — *"a soft shield silhouette split into two halves, blue and amber,
   with a small teal spark crossing the seam; minimal, app-icon ready."*
3. **Relay node** — *"three connected nodes in a triangle (owner, verifier, recipient)
   with one edge lighting up teal as a signal passes; clean line logo on navy."*
